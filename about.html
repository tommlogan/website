Tom Logan

Tom is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Industrial and Operations Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Originally from New Zealand, he completed his undergraduate studies at the University of Canterbury in Christchurch, NZ. Tom's research interests include the design of sustainable and resilient cities in order to make urban spaces more environmentally friendly and liveable. To this end, he is currently exploring a range of modelling techniques, including network theory, data analytics, and system dynamics.
