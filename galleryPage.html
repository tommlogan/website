Projects and Publications

Prediction models

Tom M Logan, Stella McLeod, Seth Guikema

If we could predict the size of fruit before harvest, growers would be really happy. Models in past have had varying success; sometimes the studies don't check how good the model is for prediction. Using data mining I build a predictive model for apples and discuss how to check how good it is.

Shearwater Migration

Tom M Logan

Shearwaters (a type of seabird) migrate huge distances every year (Arctic to Antarctic and back!). They live the perfect life of endless summer. But how do they know when to depart?
